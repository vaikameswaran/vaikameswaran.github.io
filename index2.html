Hi! My name is Vaishnav and I am a Ph.D. pre-candidate at the University of Michigan - School of Information where I work with Sile O'Modhrain, Tiffany Veinot and Joyojeet Pal. My research interests lie at the intersection of Human-Computer Interaction and Accessibility. My current work examines the relationship between technology use and socio-economic inclusion of people with visual impairments in India.

Previously, I have interned with the Enable group at Microsoft Research, Redmond and the Technology for Emerging Markets group at Microsoft Research, India.

I like swimming and playing ultimate frisbee although I am not great at either. You can get in touch with me at vaikam at umich dot edu.
